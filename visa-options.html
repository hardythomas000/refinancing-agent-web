Application Timeline

Now (Jan)

1. Document Gathering

Prepare marriage certificate (Buku Nikah), wife's KTP/KK, and your passport. Ensure all are scanned clearly.

Early Feb

2. Verify Sponsorship

Your wife must register as a sponsor on the Immigration portal. This can take a few days to approve.

March 1st

3. Submit Application

Lodge the E317 application online. Processing typically takes 5-10 working days for the E-Visa issuance.

May (Arrival)

4. Convert to KITAS

Enter Indonesia regarding the E-Visa. Then visit the local immigration office (Kantor Imigrasi) within 30 days for biometrics to get the physical KITAS.

Spouse KITAS Checklist

  • Wife's KTP (ID Card) & Kartu Keluarga (Family Card)
    Must be current and address must match.
  • Marriage Certificate (Buku Nikah)
    If married in AU, must be reported/legalized by KBRI.
  • Bank Statement (Wife's Account)
    Proof of funds (approx $2,000 USD equivalent).
  • Passports (Yours + Kids)
    Must have min. 18 months validity remaining.
  • Color Passport Photos
    Red background is traditional for standard docs.
