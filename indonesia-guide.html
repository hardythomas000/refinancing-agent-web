Opening Requirements

  • Passport & Visa: KITAS/KITAP preferred. Some branches allow B211A.
  • Domicile Letter: Proof of Indonesian address (rental contract).
  • NPWP: Tax ID (not always required for initial setup, but recommended).
  • Deposit: IDR 500k - 1M for basic accounts.

NPWP Identification Number

You are considered a tax resident if you stay 183+ days in Indonesia within a 12-month period.

Why you need an NPWP

Without an NPWP, you are taxed at a flat 20% higher rate on gross income from Indonesian sources. It's also required for KITAS renewals and long-term banking.

Key Milestones

  • Register online: Through the DGT e-registration portal once you have your KITAS.
  • Annual Filing: Personal returns are due by March 31st each year.
  • 4-Year Exemption: Ask your tax advisor about exemptions for foreign-sourced income (your rental income) for the first 4 years.
