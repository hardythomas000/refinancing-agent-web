The Spouse advantage

Since you are married to a WNI, you are exempt from most employment-based visa hurdles. Your wife is your anchor.

Action Items for Noor

  • Register as a "Personal Sponsor" on evisa.imigrasi.go.id.
  • Ensure KTP and KK addresses match perfectly.
  • Verify bank statement shows at least $2,000 USD (IDR 30jt+).

Action Items for Hardy

  • Verify passport has at least 18 months validity remaining.
  • Get digital copies of Marriage Certificate (legalized).
  • Prepare the kids' passports and birth certificates.

üöÄ Application Process

  • Noor registers as Personal Sponsor
    Must be done first on the evisa portal.
  • Lodge E317 Application
    Submit all documents and pay the fee online.
  • Receive E-Visa (PDF)
    Wait 5-10 working days for processing.
  • Arrive in Indonesia
    Enter on the E-Visa at Immigration.
  • Biometrics at Local Imigrasi
    Within 30 days of arrival to get physical KITAS.
